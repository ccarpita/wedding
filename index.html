Where?

We're excited to be having our wedding at Maple Shade Farm, located at 2066 County Hwy 18 in Delhi, NY.

Maple Shade Farm was settled in 1785 by Daniel Frisbee, son of Judge Gideon Frisbee. Gideon Frisbee was one of the early pioneers of Delaware County. Jay and Abby Wilson purchased the property in July of 2007 and are raising their three children on the farm in the County where they grew up and attended school together. The Wilson Family opened their farm to the public in September 2008 to share their passion for agriculture and education.
