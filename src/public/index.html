Getting There

The best way to get directions to Maple Shade is to use Google Maps, enter your starting location, and print them out or e-mail them to yourself.

Note that cell reception can get spotty while you're driving through the Catskills, so make sure to have your directions printed or saved offline!

The closest airport is in Binghamton (BGM), but if you're renting a car it's possible that flying to EWR, JFK, or LGA will be less costly.

Please contact us if you want to carpool or have space in your vehicle for others.
